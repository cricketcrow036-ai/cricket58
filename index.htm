    Nationality: Indian

    Profession: Former International Cricketer

    Role: Top-order Batsman

    Batting Style: Right-handed

    Nickname: GOD OF CRICKET

    • Sachin Tendulkar is one of the greatest batsmen in the history of world cricket.

    • He is known for his dedication, discipline, and technically perfect batting style.

    • Sachin holds the record for scoring 100 international centuries, a milestone unmatched in cricket.

    • He became the first cricketer to score a double century in One Day Internationals (200*).

    • His straight drive is considered one of the most beautiful shots in cricket history.

    • Sachin Tendulkar is admired and respected by millions of cricket fans around the world and is often called the "God of Cricket."
