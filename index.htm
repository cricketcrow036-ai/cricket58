    Nationality: Indian

    Role: Top-order Batsman

    Batting Style: Right-handed

    Nickname: King Kohli

    • He is known for his aggressive playing style and strong determination.

    • Kohli has scored centuries in all formats of international cricket.

    • He has captained the Indian cricket team with passion and confidence.

    • His fitness and discipline make him a role model for young athletes.

    • Virat Kohli is admired worldwide for his consistency and match-winning performances.

    Nationality: Indian

    Role: Opening Batsman

    3.SACHIN TENDULKAR

    Full Name: Sachin Ramesh Tendulkar

    Date of Birth: 24 April 1973

    Birthplace: Mumbai, Maharashtra, India

    Nationality: Indian

    Profession: Former International Cricketer

    Role: Top-order Batsman

    Batting Style: Right-handed

    Nickname: GOD OF CRICKET

    • Sachin Tendulkar is one of the greatest batsmen in the history of world cricket.

    • He is known for his dedication, discipline, and technically perfect batting style.

    • Sachin holds the record for scoring 100 international centuries, a milestone unmatched in cricket.

    • He became the first cricketer to score a double century in One Day Internationals (200*).

    • He represented India for 24 years and carried the hopes of the nation with pride and consistency.

    • His straight drive is considered one of the most beautiful shots in cricket history.

    • Sachin Tendulkar is admired and respected by millions of cricket fans around the world and is often called the "God of Cricket."

      Nationality: Indian

      • MS Dhoni is admired and respected by millions of cricket fans around the world and is fondly known as "Captain Cool."




        5.RAHUL DRAVID

        Full Name: Rahul Sharad Dravid

        Date of Birth: 11 January 1973

        Birthplace: Indore, Madhya Pradesh, India

        Nationality: Indian

        Profession: Former International Cricketer, Cricket Coach

        Role: Top-order Batsman

        Batting Style: Right-handed

        Nickname: The Wall

        • Rahul Dravid is one of the greatest batsmen in the history of Indian cricket.

        • He is known for his solid technique, patience, and strong defensive skills at the crease.

        • Dravid earned the nickname "The Wall" for his ability to stand firm against the toughest bowling attacks in world cricket.

        • He scored over 13,000 runs in both Test and One Day International cricket, making him one of the highest run-scorers for India.

        • He played a key role in many historic victories for India, especially in overseas Test matches.

        • His calm temperament and dedication to the game made him one of the most respected cricketers in the world.

        • Rahul Dravid is admired by cricket fans for his humility, discipline, and immense contribution to Indian cricket.
